Data is given in the form of paragraph in caselets questions. It is advisable to highlight or underline the important facts and figures and if possible make your own table/chart or a graph for solving these types of questions. 

Combination graph is a combination of two or more types of graphs.
In combination graph questions, a pie-chart and a line graph may be shown together in one question or a question may be a combination of bar graph, caselet and a table.
Questions are solved using the information from different graphs
