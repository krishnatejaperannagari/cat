Sentence completion questions test two separate aspects of your verbal ability: vocabulary and internal logic of sentences.
The trigger words, in comparison questions are likewise, similarly, and, just as, as _________ as, for example, as shown, as illustrated by.
The trigger words in contrast questions are though, although, however, despite, but, yet, on the other hand, despite, on the contrary.
The trigger words in restatement questions are namely, in other words, in fact, that is.
The trigger words in cause and effect questions are thus, therefore, consequently, because and phrases like due to, as a result, leads to.
